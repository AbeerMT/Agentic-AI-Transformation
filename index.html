Strategic Intent
AI transformation vision & strategic control
Before any governance, technology, or deployment decision — define why AI changes the firm and who wins because of it. This pillar governs all others. It answers where decision authority shifts, what becomes AI-native vs AI-assisted, and how AI reshapes the operating model, margins, and competitive position.

Pillar 01 · Deployment strategy
01
Phased implementation — crawl, walk, run
Govern the pace of AI deployment through a structured maturity model. Start with low-risk, non-critical use cases under full human oversight. Establish clear success gates before progressing — full autonomy is earned, not assumed.
Crawl · 0–3 months
Low-risk tasks, non-critical data, full human review on all outputs
Walk · 3–6 months
Service-level intelligence, partial automation, expanded scope with oversight
Run · 6–12 months
Full automation of proven use cases, predictive insights, scaled operations
